About Us

Quality Work, Fair Price. Nothing More.

Entrust your bike with us and you'll receive quality servicing from our experienced team. Kevin Carter has been runner-up in Honda's Mechanic of the Year, and we've served riders for over 20 years from all over Sussex and beyond.

We use only quality parts from our trusted suppliers, and if you need something specific we can often source it within a day. Our focus is quality work done, at a fair price. That means you don't pay for our marketing, fancy headed paper or parts that you don't need.

All our business is word of mouth, and doing a good job is the only marketing we do.
